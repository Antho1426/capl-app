DUAL D-PAD CONTROLLER Easter Egg

Configuration with the micro:bit of the Cardbot

Now that you have found the easter egg of the CaPL app, you can simply access to the remote control mode of the robot by using this functionality of the app together with the proper setting of your micro:bit. To experience this, simply press at least 5 times on the A button of your micro:bit. Once the index is over 5 and the skull symbol is displayed, you can begin to freely use your robot with the gamepad controller you just found!

Control instructions

Once you have correctly set up your robot, you can control it as follows: the up and down buttons of the left pad make the robot move forward and backward and the right and left buttons of the right pad make the robot turn left and right. Enjoy!

Note

If you want to get back to the original control settings of the robot that works with the CaPL app standard activities ("Free Game" and "Geography Game"), simply press the A and B buttons together. This will reset the index of the micro:bit to '0'.
